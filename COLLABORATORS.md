
## Call for Co-Authors & Collaborators

The SIGNN Protocol v0.1 is released as an open draft specification.

We are seeking co-authors, reviewers, and domain collaborators to refine and formalize this work into a publishable technical paper and cross-industry accountability framework.

Areas of collaboration include:

- AI governance & regulatory compliance (EU AI Act, high-risk AI systems)
- Applied cryptography & signature verification models
- Distributed trust and transparency log architectures
- Human-in-the-loop AI oversight models
- Healthcare and regulated industry AI deployment

Ideal collaborators:

- AI safety / governance researchers
- Security engineers with cryptographic background
- Regulatory compliance experts
- Healthcare AI system architects
- Academic researchers interested in AI accountability standards

The goal is to evolve this draft into:

- A peer-reviewable technical paper
- A domain-neutral open specification
- A practical accountability layer for high-risk AI systems

Interested collaborators may open a GitHub Issue or reach out directly.
